1. Sharing Knowledge#

With this blog I aim to share my projects, which are manifestations of my learning through my own thinking, and learning from the knowledge created by others.

I will attempt to make this blog high-quality, educational, and dense in knowledge/learning!

2. Community#

I am writing here, because I beleive that sharing with the community is a great way to motivate myself, and to truly push myself to further understand. I hope that you comment on my post, share feedback, and criticize!

3. Depth of knowledge#

The most important part of this blog is learning and deeply understanding. With every project/post, I will be asking the question: how can I dig deeper? This inevitably involves building-things out and challenging the mind with ‘mental (work)thinkouts’, and thinking through/spending time with subjects.
